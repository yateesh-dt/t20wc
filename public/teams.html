India - More Info
The Indian cricket team is governed by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI). India has won multiple ICC trophies including two World Cups. The team is known for its strong batting lineup and a variety of talented bowlers.
Australia - More Info
The Australian cricket team, managed by Cricket Australia, has been a dominant force in world cricket. Known for their aggressive play style, Australia has secured numerous ICC titles and boasts a rich history of legendary players.
England - More Info
The England cricket team is the oldest in the sport and is managed by the England and Wales Cricket Board (ECB). With a history that dates back to the 19th century, England has been a pioneer in the game and has won several major international tournaments.
Pakistan - More Info
The Pakistan cricket team, governed by the Pakistan Cricket Board (PCB), is known for its unpredictability and flair. Pakistan has won multiple ICC events, including the T20 World Cup and the Cricket World Cup, and is celebrated for its fast bowlers and skilled batsmen.
New Zealand - More Info
The New Zealand cricket team, also known as the Black Caps, is managed by New Zealand Cricket. Known for their sportsmanship and team spirit, New Zealand has consistently performed well in international cricket, reaching the finals of several major tournaments.
South Africa - More Info
The South African cricket team, known as the Proteas, is managed by Cricket South Africa. Despite facing various challenges, South Africa has produced some of the best cricketing talents and is known for its competitive edge in international cricket.
Sri Lanka - More Info
The Sri Lankan cricket team, managed by Sri Lanka Cricket, is known for its flair and resilience. Sri Lanka has won the ICC World Cup and the ICC T20 World Cup, and has produced numerous world-class cricketers over the years.
